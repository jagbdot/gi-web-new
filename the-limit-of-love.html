The limit of love

The Limit Of Love

01
Music
Jeremy Wallace Maclean.
Location
A place unkown till now with long grass.
Starring
MJ Kehler & Samuel Campbell Wilson.

The Limit of Love explores the subconscious paradox of loving eternally whilst fearing the end. The Limit of Love premiered on Directors Notes and picked up awards at film festivals around the globe. The Limit of Love was born from the dichotomy of loving and being afraid to love. A situation when you’re not giving your all to a relationship because you’re too afraid to be hurt. This flipped around to tell the story of conviction in walking away. In this case, it seems like the perfect relationship but behind the intensity of new love, there’s a greater darkness when doubt or fear comes in. This was the main driver of the short film.
