Sink or Swim

Director
Sterling Larose.
Director of Photography
Mitchell Baxter.
Starring
The Snotty Nose Rez Kids, The OBGMs, and Just John.

Sink or Swim cries to the immediacy of the impending crisis on our planet. The Snotty Nose Rez Kids are a hip-hop duo from Vancouver, BC. Sterling Larose came to GOOD-IDEA to produce this punk-performance focused video featuring The OBGMS and Just John. This was shot over the course of a day at the Rickshaw theatre with a close nit group of creatives.
