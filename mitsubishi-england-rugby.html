Mitsubishi x England Rugby

01

England Rugby

Agency
Trailer Park London.
Client
Mitsubishi UK.
Producer
Rob Wildsmith.

Shot during the height of England’s longest winning streak of all time. this series of films peel back the layers to get inside the calculated minds of world class rugby players. The film draws parallels between the different stages of strategy during a world cup match and how this simulates the design and functionality of the Mitsubishi PHEV.
