In the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. twenty four filmmakers from across Canada donated their time and talent to create a ‘love letter’ to healthcare workers. This concept came from our director, Ross Allen, and was executed by GOOD-IDEA®. We partnered with Canadahelps COVID-19 Healthcares and Hospital Fund, who helped raise over $2 million in public donations, a sum that was matched by Gore Mutual Insurance. Upon release, Heartbeat of the Nation earned a Vimeo Staff Pick, YDA Shortlist nomination, recognition at the 1.4 Awards, and international press.
