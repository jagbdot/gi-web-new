“Bad” by Harlequin Gold is the second single off of Harlequin Gold’s anticipated new Album. This music video explores the notion of self. Self-doubt, self-discovery, and self-growth. It focuses on the transition of changing how you see yourself vs how other people see you, which can happen at the most unexpected time. Elle (singer), embarks on a journey of self-discovery and although she appears to be unraveling throughout the song, and her screams ignored by the circle of judgment, she’s slowly becoming more comfortable with who she truly is. This was a really exciting space to explore. The transition of old and new.
