This national commercial aired during the Tokyo Olympics. We worked with our partners at Radke to deliver this comedic campaign for Rethink Toronto and Kraft Canada. The ambitious one take opening shot drone, was made extra challenging when you factor in how close we wanted to be to Olympic Bronze medalist Evan Dunfee. Ensuring the smooth and safe high to low wrap around with a heavy lift drone took precision, coordination, and impeccable timing with our friends at Peacemaker filmworks.
